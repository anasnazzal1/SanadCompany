Wataneyya Accelerate: Commercializing Innovation

Unlocking new revenue streams through unleashing founders' innovation

Wataneyya Accelerate is Wataneyya IT’s business accelerator, focused on identifying, investing in, and scaling high-potential innovations. Rather than building every product in-house, we fund and commercialize startup-driven solutions that align with our business strategy.

We combine investment, strategic guidance, and market access to transform startups innovation into market-ready solutions. We integrate these innovations into Iraq’s largest verticals, creating new revenue streams for Wataneyya IT and a direct path to enterprise adoption for founders.

Our Approach

Sources of great business ideas are widely distributed, and no company, no matter how capable, can afford to rely solely on itself to unlock the best opportunities. We embrace this reality by adopting a methodical approach to actively seek, absorb, and apply insights—from global practices and technologies to user needs and market trends—to stay ahead and create IT and AI products and services that meet Iraq’s evolving challenges.

We apply this approach through four structured stages:

Discovery

Actively search for ideas from internal and external sources that can spark new thinking.

Understanding

Carefully analyze what we uncover, connecting it with our existing knowledge and Iraq’s local context.

Adaptation

Refine and shape promising ideas into solutions that are relevant, practical, and aligned with client needs.

Implementation

Bring these ideas to life by launching new services, enhancing existing ones, or addressing real client challenges.
